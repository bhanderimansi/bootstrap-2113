EMCO Meditek Pvt. Ltd.






Capnograph


EMCO CapnoWave 3400 is an economical, standalone, lightweight, bedside and portable Capnograph Monitor which measures End Tidal CO2 (etCO2), Final Inspired CO2 (iCO2) and Respiration Rate. It is based on the latest state-of-artPlug& Play techonolgy from Respironics, USA.

Flexibility to choose Mainstream or Sidestream Capnography

Mainstream sensor (CAPNOSTAT 5) offered by us is a small, lightweight mainstream CO2 sensor that helps in providing accurate as well as reliable monitoring for all incubated patients from neonates to adults. Designed to deliver maximum durability, the sensors are tough enough to withstand everyday life in ICU or EMS environment. Further, the easy-to-use on-airway adapters are precision designed to optimize system performance by reducing effects of moisture, thus resulting in hassle free and uninterrupted monitoring.

Sidestream Sensor (LoFlo) is the newest 50ml sampling rate sensor that provides consistent and reliable CO2 monitoring of adult, pediatric and neonatal patients.

NIBP Monitor

  • Oscillometric method – measuring Systolic, Diastolic, MAP and Pulse Rate.
  • Body movement and Check Cuff Alarms.

The EMCO 4040 NP is an indigenously designed NIBP which incorporates the latest technologies from USA. The unit provides fast, reliable and accurate readings of Non-Invasive Blood Pressure and Pulse Rate.

Features

  • Oscillometric method – measuring Systolic, Diastolic, MAP and Pulse Rate.
  • Separate Adult and Neonate functions with separate inflation and Alarm Settings
  • User selectable initial inflation pressure
  • Body movement and Check Cuff Alarms
  • Automatic pressure release for Overpressure
  • Memory of last 99 measurements available for display and print-out
  • Wide range of cuffs available from 2.5 cms to 16 cms width
  • Easily upgradable to include Pulse Oximetry
